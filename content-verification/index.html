üéØ What is Content Verification?

A blockchain-based system that lets anyone verify if online content has been officially registered by its creator. Combat deepfakes and misinformation with cryptographic proof.

üí° The Problem We Solve

In the age of AI-generated content and deepfakes, it's increasingly difficult to verify authentic content. This tool provides immutable proof that content was registered by a specific creator at a specific time.

‚ú® Key Features

  • URL Verification: Check any URL against registered blockchain records
  • Creator Identity: See who registered the content
  • Timestamp Proof: Verify when content was registered
  • Immutable Records: Registration cannot be altered or deleted

üîç How to Verify Content

  1. Enter URL: Paste the content URL in the search box
  2. Click Verify: The system searches the blockchain
  3. View Results: See if content is registered and by whom
