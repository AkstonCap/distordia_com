üéØ What is Distordia Social?

A decentralized social media platform where every post is stored permanently on the Nexus blockchain. No central authority can delete or censor your content.

üí° True Ownership

Each post becomes a blockchain asset that you own. Your content exists forever on-chain, tied to your cryptographic identity.

‚ú® Key Features

  • Immutable Posts: Once posted, content cannot be altered or deleted
  • Namespace Identity: Posts tied to your Nexus namespace
  • No Censorship: Truly decentralized - no one can remove your posts
  • Verifiable: Anyone can verify post authenticity on blockchain

üöÄ Getting Started

  1. Connect Wallet: Click "Connect Wallet" and approve with Q-Wallet
  2. Create Post: Click "Post" and write your message (max 500 chars)
  3. Confirm Transaction: Approve the blockchain transaction in your wallet
  4. View Posts: Your post appears in the feed, stored forever on-chain
